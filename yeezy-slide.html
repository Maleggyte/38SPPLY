Yeezy Slide

Price: IDR 2,750,000

The Yeezy Slide is a minimalist slide sandal designed by Kanye West in collaboration with Adidas. Released in 2021, it features a sleek, streamlined silhouette made from lightweight EVA foam, offering exceptional comfort and cushioning. The slide is known for its soft, contoured footbed that provides a snug fit, making it ideal for casual wear or lounging.

  • Color: White/Black
  • Material: Leather Upper, Rubber Sole
  • Available Sizes: 39, 40, 41, 42, 43
