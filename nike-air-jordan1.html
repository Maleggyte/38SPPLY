Nike Air Jordan 1

Price: IDR 1,500,000

The Air Jordan 1, released in 1985, is the first model in the Air Jordan line and is iconic in both basketball and sneaker culture. Designed for Michael Jordan, it features a high-top silhouette, a distinctive colorway, and the famous Wings logo. Initially banned by the NBA for not adhering to uniform regulations, this controversy fueled its popularity. Known for its comfortable fit and stylish design, the Air Jordan 1 has become a staple in streetwear and a sought-after collectible, with numerous collaborations and limited editions released over the years.

  • Color: White/Black
  • Material: Leather Upper, Rubber Sole
  • Available Sizes: 39, 40, 41, 42, 43
