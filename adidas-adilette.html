Adidas Adillete

Price: IDR 800,000

The Adidas Adilette is a classic slide sandal that was first introduced in the 1970s. Known for its simple and functional design, it features a comfortable footbed and a single or double strap made of soft synthetic materials. The Adilette is popular for its versatility, making it suitable for both casual wear and after-sport relaxation.

  • Color: White/Black
  • Material: Leather Upper, Rubber Sole
  • Available Sizes: 39, 40, 41, 42, 43
