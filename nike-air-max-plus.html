Nike Air Max Plus

Price: IDR 2,200,000

The Nike Air Max Plus, also known as the TN (Tuned Air), was released in 1998 and is recognized for its distinctive design and cushioning technology. It features a unique upper with wavy lines and a pronounced heel, giving it a bold, sporty look. The shoe incorporates Tuned Air technology for enhanced cushioning and support, providing a comfortable fit for both athletic and casual wear.

  • Color: White/Black
  • Material: Leather Upper, Rubber Sole
  • Available Sizes: 39, 40, 41, 42, 43
