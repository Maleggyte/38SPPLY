Adidas Alphabounce

Price: IDR 750,000

The Adidas Alphabounce is a running shoe designed for comfort and versatility. Launched in 2016, it features a Bounce midsole that provides responsive cushioning and energy return, making it suitable for various types of workouts. The shoe has a seamless, breathable upper that offers a snug fit and flexibility, enhancing overall performance. Its durable outsole provides traction on different surfaces, making it a great choice for both running and cross-training.

  • Color: White/Black
  • Material: Leather Upper, Rubber Sole
  • Available Sizes: 39, 40, 41, 42, 43
