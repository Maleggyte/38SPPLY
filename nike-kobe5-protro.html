Nike Kobe 5 Protro

Price: IDR 2,800,000

The Nike Kobe 5 Protro is a modernized version of Kobe Bryant's fifth signature basketball shoe, originally released in 2010. The "Protro" version enhances performance with updated technology, including improved cushioning, lighter materials, and better support, while maintaining the original design. It's popular among basketball players for its sleek look and on-court functionality.

  • Color: White/Black
  • Material: Leather Upper, Rubber Sole
  • Available Sizes: 39, 40, 41, 42, 43
