Yeezy 350

Price: IDR 1,800,000

The Yeezy 350, designed by Kanye West in collaboration with Adidas, was released in 2015 and quickly became a cultural phenomenon. Known for its sleek, minimalist design, it features a Primeknit upper for comfort and flexibility, along with a distinctive Boost sole for cushioning. The shoe is recognized for its unique colorways and patterns, making each release highly sought after.

  • Color: White/Black
  • Material: Leather Upper, Rubber Sole
  • Available Sizes: 39, 40, 41, 42, 43
